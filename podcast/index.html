IDT Podcast

It is important for students to hear from industry. Guest lectures can be great, but they eat a lot of time. What if students got to listen to many industry professionals, in shorter pre-recorded interviews, then they could choose who they want to hear more from later?

That's what this is.

Format

  • Virtual Meeting (Zoom)
  • 10-15 Minutes
  • ~6 Questions

Example Questions

  • What is your role?
  • What are your primary responsibilities?
  • What tools / software do you use?
  • What does your team's process look like?
  • What does a typical day look like for you?
  • What are important skills to have for your role?
  • What is some advice you'd give to students?
