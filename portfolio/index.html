About

I'm an educator and software developer focused on front-end development, mobile applications, and UI/UX design. I've helped launch dozens of websites, ship hundreds of apps/games, and taught students about technology, computer science, programming, and design. My teaching philosophy is rooted in Universal Design for Learning, Work-integrated Learning, and demystifying technology. Currently I am in Instructor at Saskatchewan Polytechnic in the Interactive Design & Technology program.

I struggled at the University of Saskatchewan, but everything turned around in 2007 when I was accepted to the internship program. I landed a job at IBM and moved to Toronto for 16 months. I completed my B.Sc. Computer Science shortly after returning.

My professional career began in 2009 as a Web Developer at zu, a rapidly growing creative agency in Saskatoon. I grew into being the Team Lead for the mobile app development team and helped ship several iOS apps.

In 2012 I jumped at the opportunity to join a small startup called Noodlecake. Starting at a Senior Developer I helped the team port and publish several mobile games. As the company grew, I filled the role of Director of Publishing, overseeing the release of dozens more games.

In early 2018 I started sessional lecturing at the University of Saskatchewan in the department of Computer Science part time. I had always like presenting and felt passionately about democrotizing basic computer programming knowledge. Throughout the next few years I taught first year and some third year courses.

Feeling tired and burnt out after Noodlecake was acquired, in late 2018 I landed at another creative agency. At Territorial I lead the development of a mental health support app and a mobile storytelling platform.

Finally in 2021 I found myself at Saskatchewan Polytechnic teaching Interactive Design & Technology. The role has provided me the opporunity to bring my various experiences and career knowledge into the classroom. Through the Learners, Instruction & Fundamentals of Teaching (LIFT) program I have been able to hone my skills as an instructor.

For more background information feel free to check out my LinkedIn profile or education website.
